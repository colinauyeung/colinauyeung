About Me

Hello, my name is Colin Au Yeung. I am an undergraduate student in computer science at the University of Calgary. My studies have focused on interaction design. I am currently doing undergraduate research under Dr. Lora Oelhberg focusing on understanding and designing technologies for supporting creative communities. I enjoy understanding how people interact with the world and building new and novel ways to support those paths. I want to provide people with new experiences that better connects them with others and showcase their skills to wider audiences. I hope to build technology that better support real people.
